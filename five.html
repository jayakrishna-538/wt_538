    • INTRODUCTION





    Once there lived a Hare and a Tortoise in the
    forest. It was a speedy hare who bragged about how fast he
    could run. All the animals were tired of hearing him boast.
    One day the tortoise wanted to teach him a lesson, so the
    tortoise challanged him to a race.

    The challange is one who reaches the tree, wins the race. The Hare accepte this challange. All the animals in the forest gathered to watch the race.

    The hare ran down the road and then look back at the tortoise and cried out, "How do u expect to win these race when you are walking along at your slow pace?"

    The hare thought that it has already completed half of the race and could easily finish the rest. So it stretched him self out along side the road and fell a sleep.

    The tortoise didn't took rest, it just kept on walking and walking without stoping. Slowly has crossed sleeping hare.

    All the animals started cheering the tortoise so loudly that made the hare to wake up. The hare saw that the tortoise has already crossed him so it began to run.

    But the hare was too late because the tortoise is already on the finish line.

    Atlast, the tortoise won the race and the hare felt bad for his negligence all the animals were happy and congratulated the tortoise
